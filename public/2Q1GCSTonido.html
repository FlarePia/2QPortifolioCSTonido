The Background of Leni Robredo:

The History of an Admirable Filipino

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Leni Robredo that we know today is a woman who fights for her beliefs and what is best for the country. But we must discover her journey and take a look at the life of Leni Robredo

Leni was born on April 23, 1965 in Naga, Camarines Sur.

But on an unexpected day,Leni's husband, Jesse Robredo died in a plane crash in 2012. Leaving her the only parent of their 3 daughters: Janine, Jillian and Jessica. Only after her husband's death did she start getting involved in politics, a story that continues to be told until this day
